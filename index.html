The Mathematics Research Center, A.C. (CIMAT) is a public research center integrated into the CONACYT Public Research Center System. It is dedicated to the generation, transmission, and application of specialized knowledge in the areas of mathematics, statistics, and computer science. Oriented towards scientific research, the training of high-level human resources, the improvement of society's mathematical competence, as well as support in solving problems that fall within its areas of interest, CIMAT seeks to contribute to the scientific and technological development of Mexico.

Dr. Adrián Pastor
López Monroy

Director Associate Professor at CIMAT-Gto


    Dr. Adrián Pastor López Monroy received his Master's and Doctor's degrees in Computer Science from the Instituto Nacional de Astrofísica, Óptica y Electrónica (INAOE, Mexico) in 2012 and 2017, respectively. In 2017, he received the award for the best doctoral thesis in Artificial Intelligence (AI) from the Mexican Society of AI. Subsequently, in 2017 and 2018, he was a Full-Time Associate Researcher and Lecturer at the University of Houston, in Texas, USA. Currently, Dr. López Monroy is a Full-Time Research Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the Centro de Investigación en Matemáticas (CIMAT, Mexico). His research areas are Deep Learning for Natural Language Processing, and since 2013 he has led participations in machine learning competitions in the European forums "Conference and Labs of the Evaluation Forum CLEF-PAN" and in the "Iberian Language Evaluation Forum IberLEF", winning first place in various editions in Spain, England, France and Greece. He is a member of the Sistema Nacional de Investigadores (SNI) and CONAHCyT in groups for artificial intelligence (e.g., Alianza-IA, RedICA), and language technologies (RedTTL). He currently has more than 60 refereed publications in journals, conferences and book chapters, as well as more than 1300 citations and an H-index of 19 on Google Scholar.

Dr. Fernando
Sánchez Vega

Researcher appointed at CIMAT-Gto by CONAHCyT



    Fernando Sánchez-Vega obtained his MS and PhD degrees in Computer Science from the National Institute for Astrophysics, Optics and Electronics (INAOE, Mexico) in 2010 and 2016, respectively. He received the Young Researchers Award from the Center for Advanced Studies in Information and Communication Technologies (CEATIC) at the University of Jaen (UJaen), Spain in 2017. In 2020, he held a Postdoctoral Fellow position in the Department of Computer Science at the Mathematics Research Center (CIMAT, Mexico) and another in the Data Sciences Program in Humanities at the Colegio de Mexico (COLMEX) in 2021. Since 2022, Dr. Sánchez-Vega has been a Professor in the Department of Computer Science at CIMAT, Mexico. His research topics of interest include deep learning, natural language processing, language and vision, and data sciences. He regularly collaborates with multiple research groups, including the "Lexicon, Dictionary, and Information Technology" Laboratory (LDI) at Université Paris Nord, France, and the Natural Language Engineering Lab at the Pattern Recognition and Human Language Technologies (PRHLT) Research Center in Spain, Language Technologies Laboratory (LabTL) at INAOE, Mexico, among others.
